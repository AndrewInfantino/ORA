Our Story

A Passion for Matcha.
A Purpose in Every Can.

We're a married couple who love matcha, but no matter where we looked, we couldn't find a ready-to-drink matcha latte that was accessible, consistent, beautiful, and truly delicious.

After travelling the world and experiencing tea culture in places like China and Japan, we fell in love with the ritual, quality, and calm energy behind matcha.

ORA was born to bring that same feeling into a ready-to-drink format that fits modern life.
